# Solution 7: Wavelength and Frequency

## Problem Statement
The human eye is most sensitive to light with a wavelength of about $550 \text{ nm}$. What color does this correspond to in the visible spectrum? What is the frequency of this light?

## Step-by-Step Solution

**1. Determine the color:**
The visible spectrum of light ranges from approximately $380 \text{ nm}$ (violet) to $750 \text{ nm}$ (red).
* A wavelength of $550 \text{ nm}$ lies right in the middle of the visible spectrum.
* Based on the color spectrum, $550 \text{ nm}$ corresponds to a **yellowish-green** color (or just **green**).

**2. Calculate the frequency:**
The frequency $f$ of an electromagnetic wave is related to its wavelength $\lambda$ and the speed of light $c$ by the equation:
$$ c = \lambda f $$
Solving for $f$:
$$ f = \frac{c}{\lambda} $$
where:
* $c \approx 3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$
* $\lambda = 550 \text{ nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9} \text{ m} = 5.5 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$

Substitute the values:
$$ f = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}}{5.5 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}} $$
$$ f = \frac{3.0}{5.5} \times 10^{15} \text{ Hz} \approx 0.545 \times 10^{15} \text{ Hz} $$
$$ f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz} $$

**Final Answer:**
* The color corresponding to $550 \text{ nm}$ is **green** (or yellowish-green).
* The frequency of this light is approximately **$5.45 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$** (or $545 \text{ THz}$).
