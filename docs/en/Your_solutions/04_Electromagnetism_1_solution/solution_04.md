# 4. Force Comparison

**Problem:**
Calculate the magnitude of the electric force and the gravitational force between an electron and a proton in a hydrogen atom (average distance $r \approx 5.3 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m}$). What is the ratio $F_e/F_g$?

**Solution:**
We are given the following constants and values:
* Average distance: $r \approx 5.3 \times 10^{-11}\text{ m}$
* Elementary charge: $e \approx 1.6 \times 10^{-19}\text{ C}$
* Coulomb's constant: $k \approx 8.99 \times 10^9\text{ N m}^2/\text{C}^2$
* Gravitational constant: $G \approx 6.67 \times 10^{-11}\text{ N m}^2/\text{kg}^2$
* Mass of electron: $m_e \approx 9.11 \times 10^{-31}\text{ kg}$
* Mass of proton: $m_p \approx 1.67 \times 10^{-27}\text{ kg}$

**1. Electric Force ($F_e$)**
Using Coulomb's law, the magnitude of the electric force is:
$$ F_e = k \frac{|q_e| |q_p|}{r^2} = k \frac{e^2}{r^2} $$
$$ F_e = (8.99 \times 10^9) \frac{(1.6 \times 10^{-19})^2}{(5.3 \times 10^{-11})^2} $$
$$ F_e = (8.99 \times 10^9) \frac{2.56 \times 10^{-38}}{28.09 \times 10^{-22}} $$
$$ F_e = \frac{23.0144 \times 10^{-29}}{28.09 \times 10^{-22}} \approx 8.2 \times 10^{-8}\text{ N} $$

**2. Gravitational Force ($F_g$)**
Using Newton's law of universal gravitation, the magnitude of the gravitational force is:
$$ F_g = G \frac{m_e m_p}{r^2} $$
$$ F_g = (6.67 \times 10^{-11}) \frac{(9.11 \times 10^{-31})(1.67 \times 10^{-27})}{(5.3 \times 10^{-11})^2} $$
$$ F_g = (6.67 \times 10^{-11}) \frac{15.2137 \times 10^{-58}}{28.09 \times 10^{-22}} $$
$$ F_g = \frac{101.475 \times 10^{-69}}{28.09 \times 10^{-22}} \approx 3.6 \times 10^{-47}\text{ N} $$

**3. Ratio ($F_e / F_g$)**
$$ \frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{8.19 \times 10^{-8}}{3.61 \times 10^{-47}} \approx 2.27 \times 10^{39} $$
Alternatively, computing the ratio analytically:
$$ \frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{k e^2}{G m_e m_p} $$
$$ \frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{(8.99 \times 10^9) (1.6 \times 10^{-19})^2}{(6.67 \times 10^{-11}) (9.11 \times 10^{-31}) (1.67 \times 10^{-27})} \approx 2.3 \times 10^{39} $$

**Answer:**
* Electric Force: $F_e \approx \mathbf{8.2 \times 10^{-8}\text{ N}}$
* Gravitational Force: $F_g \approx \mathbf{3.6 \times 10^{-47}\text{ N}}$
* Ratio: $F_e / F_g \approx \mathbf{2.3 \times 10^{39}}$
