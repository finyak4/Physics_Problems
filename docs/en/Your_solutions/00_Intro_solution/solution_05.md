## 5. Trigonometry

A vector $\vec{A}$ has a magnitude of $15$ and makes an angle of $\theta = 60^\circ$ with the horizontal axis. Calculate its horizontal and vertical components.

**Step-by-step solution:**

1. The horizontal component $A_x$ is given by $A_x = A \cos\theta$.

   $A_x = 15 \cdot \cos(60^\circ) = 15 \cdot \frac{1}{2} = 7.5$

2. The vertical component $A_y$ is given by $A_y = A \sin\theta$.

   $A_y = 15 \cdot \sin(60^\circ) = 15 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = 7.5\sqrt{3} \approx 12.99$

**Answer:** $A_x = 7.5$, $A_y = 7.5\sqrt{3} \approx 12.99$
