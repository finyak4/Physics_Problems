## 2. Systems of Equations

Find the values of $x$ and $y$ that satisfy both equations: $2x + 3y = 12$ and $x - y = 1$.

**Step-by-step solution:**

1. From the second equation, express $x$ in terms of $y$: 

   $x = y + 1$

2. Substitute this expression into the first equation:

   $2(y + 1) + 3y = 12$

3. Distribute and simplify:

   $2y + 2 + 3y = 12$

   $5y + 2 = 12$
   
   $5y = 10 \implies y = 2$

4. Substitute the value of $y$ back into $x = y + 1$:

   $x = 2 + 1 = 3$

**Answer:** $x = 3, y = 2$
