# 5. Echo Ranging

## Problem Statement
A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo 1 seconds later. How far away is the cliff? (Speed of sound in air is 343 m/s).

## Step-by-Step Solution

1. Let $d$ be the one-way distance from the person to the cliff.

2. Since the person hears an echo, the sound wave must travel all the way to the cliff, reflect, and travel all the way back.
   The **total distance** traveled by the sound is $2d$.

3. The relationship between distance, constant speed ($v$), and travel time ($t$) is:
   $$ \text{Total Distance} = v \cdot t $$
   $$ 2d = v \cdot t $$

4. We are given:
   - Speed of sound $v = 343\text{ m/s}$
   - Total travel time $t = 1\text{ s}$

5. Substitute the values:
   $$ 2d = 343\text{ m/s} \times 1\text{ s} = 343\text{ m} $$

6. Solve for $d$ to find distance to the cliff:
   $$ d = \frac{343\text{ m}}{2} = 171.5\text{ m} $$

   The cliff is 171.5 meters away.
